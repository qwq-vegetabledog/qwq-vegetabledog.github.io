# Simplex; 
k-simplex: a set of (k+1) distinct vertices; plural: simplices.\
exemple: {3, 4, 2}.\
a face: Any nonempty subset of simplex.\
simplicial complex: a collection of simplices $K$
if for every $\sigma \in K$, every face $\sigma' \subseteq \sigma
$ is also containted in $K$.\
Subcomplex: a subset that is also a simplicial complex
